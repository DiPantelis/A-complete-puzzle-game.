Easy mode:
The star-image can be swapped
with any picture inside the grid.
Time limit-> 2 minutes.
Normal mode:
The star-image can be swapped
only with the pictures inside the grid that are next to it, as shown in the example.
Time limit-> 8 minutes.
Hard mode:
The star-image can be swapped
only with the picture inside the grid that are next to it horizontally or vertically.
Time limit-> 20 minutes.

The goal in every case is to put the picture's pieces in order so that it matches with the original picture.
